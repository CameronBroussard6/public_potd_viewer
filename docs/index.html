Fritz made finals in both Stuttgart and Halle, only losing out to some really special performances from Shelton and Tiafoe, and he made a wise decision to skip the final tune-up in Eastbourne. He's dropped some sets over the course of this grass-court season, but the reality is that all of those were to better grass-courters than Lajovic. Lajovic's one-handed backhand comes with a pretty long backswing, which will make it even harder to make inroads into the Fritz serve.
