The over needs a more specific script either BABIP running hot against him, extended innings with traffic, or multiple hitters outperforming their baseline at once. That’s why the under has the cleaner path. Fried can pitch his normal game and still get there, while the over needs things to break a certain way. When you combine an elite contact suppressor with a lineup that doesn’t consistently generate hits, this is exactly the type of spot where the under tends to come through.
